The Sports Hub is a dedicated dashboard for sports enthusiasts, aggregating live scores, upcoming schedules, and league-specific data from ESPN.

## Personalized Experience

### Favorite Teams
You can follow specific teams to have them appear prominently in the **Teams** tab:
1. Navigate to the **Leagues** section.
2. Select your league and find your team.
3. Mark them as a favorite to track their progress throughout the season.

### Hub Settings
Access the **Settings** gear within the Sports Hub to customize your view:
- **Display Preferences**: Toggle which leagues and stats are shown.
- **Auto-Update**: The "Live Now" page automatically refreshes every 30 seconds during active games to ensure scores are always current.

## Viewing Features

### In-Page Streaming

### Live Coverage
The **Live Now** tab provides a real-time overview of all ongoing games across supported leagues. If a game is currently being broadcast by one of your providers, you can often jump directly to that channel from the scoreboard.
